Our FACULTY

Prof Dr Owais Khan

(Faculty of Sciences)

The Faculty of Sciences at AIOU was first established in 1982 only with five departments and limited number of undergraduate and basic functional courses. Over last three to four years, five new departments and a range of higher degree programmes have been added to the portfolio of Faculty of Sciences. Most of the departments, in the Faculty, are now engaged in the offering highly technical, scientific and professional courses.

Prof Dr Ali Imran

(Faculty of Arabic and Islamic)

The Faculty of Arabic and Islamic Studies started functioning as an Institute of Arabic and Islamic Studies comprising departments of Arabic and Islamic Studies. The Institute of Arabic and Islamic Studies was upgraded as a full-fledged faculty in 1998.

Prof Dr Rameesha

(Faculty of Education)

The Faculty of Education established in 1984 is an outgrowth of the Institute of Education, which came into existence in 1976 right after the establishment of the university. It is one of the largest faculties of the university with 47% enrolment of the university and contributor of 53% to the total annual university exchequer. At present 30 programmes and 135 courses in its eight department/Institutes are being run by the faculty
